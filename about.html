About SMT-COMP 2019

The 13th International Satisfiability Modulo Theories Competition (SMT-COMP 2019) is part of the SMT Workshop 2019, co-located with SAT 2019, Lisbon, Portugal.

The SMT Workshop will include a block of time to present the competitors and results of the competition.
